Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time. Changes will take effect on your next billing cycle.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a Free plan with limited features that you can use indefinitely. If you want to experience premium features, you can upgrade to a paid plan at any time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and various local payment methods through our secure payment processor, FastSpring.

Can I use JAAT-AI for commercial purposes?

Yes, all our paid plans can be used for commercial purposes. The Pro and Ultimate plans are particularly suitable for professional and business use.

What happens if I exceed my message limit?

On the Free and Basic plans, once you reach your monthly message limit, you'll need to wait until your next billing cycle or upgrade to a higher plan to continue using the service.
