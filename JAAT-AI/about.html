About JAAT-AI

The story behind the most animated and intelligent AI assistant

Our Story

2021

The Beginning

JAAT-AI began as a research project at the intersection of artificial intelligence and user experience design. Our founders, experienced AI researchers and UX designers, believed that AI interactions should be more engaging, animated, and personalized.

2022

Beta Launch

After a year of development, we launched our beta with the first 10 AI modes and basic animations. The response from our early users exceeded our expectations, with many highlighting the unique animated interface as a standout feature.

2023

Expansion & Growth

JAAT-AI expanded to 45+ specialized AI modes, introduced premium animations, and launched voice capabilities. Our user base grew to over 50,000 users worldwide, and we secured funding to accelerate our development.

Today

Leading Innovation

Today, JAAT-AI is recognized as the most animated and intelligent AI assistant, with users in over 30 countries. We continue to push the boundaries of AI interaction design, creating experiences that are not just helpful, but engaging and delightful.
