Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

How do I upgrade my subscription?

Log in to your account and navigate to the Dashboard. Click on "Subscription" in the sidebar menu, then select "Upgrade Plan". Choose your desired plan and follow the payment instructions.

Can I use JAAT-AI offline?

Currently, JAAT-AI requires an internet connection to operate. We're developing an offline mode for Premium and Enterprise users, which is scheduled for release in Q4 2023.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, go to your Dashboard and navigate to "Subscription" in the sidebar menu. Click on "Manage Subscription" and then "Cancel Subscription". Follow the prompts to complete the cancellation process.

Is my data secure with JAAT-AI?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All communication with our servers is encrypted using SSL, and we never share your personal data with third parties. For more information, please review our Privacy Policy.

How do I request a refund?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers. To request a refund, contact our support team with your account details within 7 days of your initial subscription purchase. For detailed information, please review our Refund Policy.
