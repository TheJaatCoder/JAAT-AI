Here are some ideas to make Lord Obsidian a more complex villain:

  1. Tragic Backstory: Perhaps he was once a protector of the realm who made a difficult choice that led to personal loss. Maybe he lost someone he loved to the same power he now seeks to control.
  2. Noble Goal, Twisted Methods: His ultimate aim could be something positive (preventing a greater catastrophe, protecting the realm from an outside threat), but his methods have become corrupted over time.
  3. Personal Connection: Consider giving him a personal connection to Elara—maybe he was her mentor, a family friend, or even a distant relative.
  4. Justified Grievance: He might have a legitimate grievance against the current order. Perhaps the Crystal Citadel's leaders once betrayed him or covered up a truth he discovered.
  5. Inner Conflict: Show moments where he questions his own path but feels too committed or desperate to turn back.
