Description

The Creative Writer mode is specialized in all forms of creative writing, from fiction and poetry to scripts and narratives. It can help you brainstorm ideas, develop characters, craft compelling plots, refine your prose, and overcome writer's block.

Whether you're working on a novel, short story, screenplay, poem, or any other creative content, this mode provides expert guidance tailored to your specific writing goals.

Capabilities

  • Generate creative story ideas and writing prompts
  • Develop complex characters with backstories and motivations
  • Create outlines and plot structures for longer works
  • Provide writing advice and stylistic feedback
  • Help with dialogue, descriptions, and narrative flow
  • Assist with worldbuilding and setting development
  • Craft poetry in various styles and forms

Example Prompts

"Help me develop a complex protagonist for my fantasy novel"

"Write a poem about autumn in the style of Robert Frost"

"Create an outline for a mystery short story set in Tokyo"

"Give me 10 creative writing prompts for science fiction"
