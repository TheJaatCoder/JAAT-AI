Basic Commands

  • "Hey JAAT" - Wake up the assistant
  • "Stop listening" - Pause voice recognition
  • "Start listening" - Resume voice recognition
  • "Mute" - Disable AI voice responses
  • "Unmute" - Enable AI voice responses

Navigation Commands

  • "Go to Dashboard" - Navigate to Dashboard
  • "Open Settings" - Open settings page
  • "Show AI Modes" - View available AI modes
  • "Switch to [Mode Name]" - Change AI mode

Content Commands

  • "Clear conversation" - Clear current chat
  • "Save conversation" - Save current chat
  • "Read back last response" - Repeat AI response
  • "Summarize conversation" - Get a summary

With voice commands in JAAT-AI, you can:

  • Ask questions and get spoken responses
  • Navigate the interface by saying "Go to Dashboard" or "Open Settings"
  • Control the conversation with commands like "Clear conversation" or "Save conversation"
  • Switch between AI modes by saying "Switch to Coder Mode" or other mode names
  • Adjust voice settings with "Speak faster," "Speak slower," or "Change voice"
  • Activate features like "Start recording," "Take notes," or "Summarize this"

You can see a full list of available commands in the sidebar on this page. Just start with "Hey JAAT" and then speak your command.
