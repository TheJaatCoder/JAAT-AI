Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about JAAT-AI

How do I create an account?

Creating an account with JAAT-AI is simple:

  1. Click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button on the homepage or login page
  2. Enter your email address, create a password, and provide a username
  3. Click "Create Account" to complete registration
  4. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox

You can also sign up using your Google, Facebook, or Apple account for a faster registration process.

What payment methods do you accept?

JAAT-AI accepts the following payment methods:

  • All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay

For annual business plans, we also accept bank transfers. Please contact our sales team for more information.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Log in to your JAAT-AI account
  2. Go to "User Profile" by clicking on your avatar in the top right
  3. Select the "Subscription" tab
  4. Click on "Manage Subscription"
  5. Select "Cancel Subscription" and follow the prompts

Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period. After cancellation, your account will revert to the free Basic plan.

Note: If you subscribed through the App Store or Google Play, you'll need to cancel your subscription through those platforms.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new premium subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with your premium experience, you can request a full refund within the first 7 days of your subscription.

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team through the Contact page
  2. Provide your account email and reason for the refund
  3. Our team will process your request within 2-3 business days

For more details, please refer to our Refund Policy.

What are AI Modes and how do I use them?

AI Modes are specialized personas and capabilities that optimize JAAT-AI for specific tasks and use cases. Each mode has unique expertise, knowledge, and interaction style.

To use AI Modes:

  1. Click on the mode selector in the chat interface (look for the icon in the top right of the chat window)
  2. Browse available modes by category or search for specific ones
  3. Select the mode you wish to use
  4. Start a new conversation with that mode active

You can also switch modes during an existing conversation by clicking the mode icon at any time.

The number of available modes depends on your subscription plan. Basic users have access to 3 modes, while premium users can access up to 45+ specialized modes.

How do I use the Voice Assistant?

To use the Voice Assistant feature:

  1. Click on the microphone icon in the chat input area or navigate to the Voice Assistant page
  2. Allow microphone permissions when prompted
  3. Speak your query after the "Listening..." indicator appears
  4. The AI will process your speech and provide a spoken response

For a hands-free experience, enable the "Wake Word" feature in Voice Assistant settings. When enabled, you can start a voice interaction by saying "Hey JAAT" followed by your question.

Voice Assistant supports 25+ languages and can be customized with different voices, speaking rates, and interaction styles in the settings.

What is Multi-Chat and how does it work?

Multi-Chat is a feature that allows you to chat with multiple AI models simultaneously and compare their responses side-by-side. This is particularly useful for:

  • Getting diverse perspectives on a question or topic
  • Comparing different AI models' approaches to a problem
  • Conducting research with multiple specialized assistants at once

To use Multi-Chat:

  1. Navigate to the Multi-Chat page from the main menu or dashboard
  2. Select the AI models you want to include in your conversation
  3. Type your message once, and all selected models will respond
  4. View all responses in the unified view or switch to individual model tabs

The number of simultaneous models you can chat with depends on your subscription plan. Bronze plan users can chat with up to 3 models, Silver users with 5 models, and Gold/Platinum users with all available models.

Why is the AI responding slowly?

Slow response times can be caused by several factors:

  • Internet Connection: A weak or unstable internet connection can affect response times. Try switching to a more stable connection.
  • High Traffic: During peak usage times, our servers may experience higher loads, which can occasionally cause delays.
  • Complex Queries: Very complex or lengthy questions may take longer to process as they require more computational resources.
  • Browser Issues: Having too many browser tabs open or outdated browsers can affect performance. Try closing unnecessary tabs or updating your browser.

To improve response times:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try the desktop app instead of the web version
  • Break very complex queries into smaller, more specific questions
  • Upgrade to a premium plan for priority server access

If you continue to experience slow responses, please contact our support team for assistance.

How do I fix microphone issues with Voice Assistant?

If you're experiencing issues with the microphone in Voice Assistant:

  1. Check Browser Permissions: Make sure you've allowed microphone access in your browser settings. Look for the microphone icon in your address bar.
  2. Verify Device Settings: Check that your microphone is correctly selected in your device settings and not muted.
  3. Test Your Microphone: Use your system's microphone test tool to verify it's working properly.
  4. Close Other Apps: Close other applications that might be using the microphone.
  5. Try Different Browser: If the issue persists, try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.).
  6. Restart Your Device: Sometimes a simple restart can resolve hardware access issues.

For Windows-specific issues:

  • Go to Settings > Privacy > Microphone and ensure microphone access is enabled for browsers

For Mac-specific issues:

  • Go to System Preferences > Security & Privacy > Privacy > Microphone and ensure your browser is checked

If none of these solutions work, try the Desktop App which sometimes has better hardware access than browsers.

Is my conversation data private?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Here's how your conversation data is handled:

  • All conversations are encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption
  • Your conversation history is stored securely in your account and is only accessible to you
  • By default, we use anonymized conversation data to improve our AI models, but you can opt out of this in your privacy settings
  • We do not sell your personal data or conversation history to third parties
  • You can delete individual conversations or your entire chat history at any time through your account settings

For enhanced privacy, premium users can enable "Private Mode" for specific conversations, which ensures that those conversations are not used for AI training and are automatically deleted after a set period.

For full details on how we handle your data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

How secure is my payment information?

Your payment information is fully secure with JAAT-AI:

  • We do not store your complete credit card details on our servers
  • All payment processing is handled by industry-leading payment processors (Stripe and PayPal) that comply with PCI DSS Level 1 standards
  • Payment data is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ during transmission
  • For subscription management, we only store tokenized versions of your payment method, not the actual card details

Our payment systems undergo regular security audits and penetration testing to ensure they remain secure against emerging threats.

Can I switch between premium plans?

Yes, you can easily upgrade or downgrade your premium plan at any time through your account settings.

Upgrading Your Plan:

  • When you upgrade to a higher-tier plan, the change takes effect immediately
  • You'll be charged the prorated difference between your current plan and the new plan for the remainder of your billing cycle
  • Your next billing date remains the same

Downgrading Your Plan:

  • When you downgrade to a lower-tier plan, the change takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle
  • You'll continue to have access to your current plan's features until the end of the billing period
  • No refunds are provided for downgrading mid-cycle (except within the 7-day refund period for new subscriptions)

To change your plan:

  1. Go to your User Profile
  2. Navigate to the Subscription tab
  3. Click "Change Plan"
  4. Select your desired plan and confirm the change

If you have any questions about changing plans or need assistance, please contact our support team.
