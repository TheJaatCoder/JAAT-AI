Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2023

At JAAT-AI, we strive to ensure your satisfaction with our services. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds related to subscriptions and purchases made through our platform.

By subscribing to or purchasing any services offered by JAAT-AI, you agree to this Refund Policy. Please read this policy carefully before making any payments.

1. Subscription Refunds

JAAT-AI offers subscription-based services with the following refund terms:

1.1 Cooling-off Period

For first-time subscribers, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee from the date of initial subscription purchase. If you are not satisfied with our service within the first 7 days, you may request a full refund by contacting our support team at support@jaat-ai.com.

To be eligible for a refund during the cooling-off period, you must:

  • Make the request within 7 days of the initial subscription purchase
  • Provide a reason for your dissatisfaction with the service
  • Have not violated our Terms of Service

1.2 Post Cooling-off Period

After the 7-day cooling-off period, subscriptions are generally non-refundable for the current billing cycle. However, you may cancel your subscription at any time to prevent future billing cycles.

When you cancel your subscription:

  • You will continue to have access to the service until the end of your current billing cycle
  • No further charges will be made for subsequent billing periods
  • No partial refunds will be issued for the unused portion of the current billing period

2. Exceptional Circumstances

We understand that exceptional circumstances may arise. We may consider refund requests outside the cooling-off period in the following situations:

2.1 Service Unavailability

If our service experiences significant downtime (more than 24 consecutive hours) or persistent technical issues that prevent you from using the service as advertised, you may be eligible for a partial or full refund for the affected period. Such circumstances will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

2.2 Billing Errors

If you have been charged incorrectly or multiple times for your subscription, please contact our support team immediately with documentation of the error. We will investigate the issue and process a refund for any incorrect charges.

2.3 Unauthorized Charges

If you discover unauthorized charges for JAAT-AI services on your account, please contact us immediately. We take security very seriously and will investigate such claims thoroughly.

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds under any circumstances:

  • Subscription fees for accounts that have been suspended or terminated due to violations of our Terms of Service
  • Charges for one-time services that have already been fulfilled
  • Fees for custom enterprise solutions or custom development work that has already been delivered
  • Additional add-on features or services purchased separately from the main subscription

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at support@jaat-ai.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Include your account details, the date of purchase, and the reason for your refund request
  3. Provide any relevant documentation or evidence to support your request

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days. If your refund is approved, it will be processed within 5-10 business days, depending on your payment method.

4.1 Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refunds will be credited back to the original card
  • UPI: Refunds will be processed to the same UPI ID used for payment
  • PayPal: Refunds will be processed to your PayPal account

Please note that depending on your payment provider, it may take additional time for the refund to appear in your account after we have processed it.

5. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team. Upon cancellation:

  • You will retain access to premium features until the end of your current billing cycle
  • No further charges will be made
  • Your account will automatically revert to the free plan at the end of the billing period
  • Any data associated with premium features may become inaccessible after your subscription ends

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Log in to your JAAT-AI account
  2. Navigate to Account Settings > Subscription
  3. Select "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm cancellation

6. Free Trial Conversions

If you signed up for a free trial that converted to a paid subscription, please note:

  • The 7-day cooling-off period begins on the date of the first payment, not the start of the free trial
  • It is your responsibility to cancel the subscription before the free trial ends if you do not wish to continue
  • We may send notifications before the trial ends, but you should not rely solely on these reminders

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes.

Substantial changes to this policy will be notified to users through email or by a prominent notice on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: support@jaat-ai.com
  • Through our support page: https://jaat-ai.com/support
  • By mail: JAAT-AI Headquarters, 123 AI Boulevard, Tech City, TX 75001, USA

We strive to address all queries within 2 business days.
