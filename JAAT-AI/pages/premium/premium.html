Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to a premium plan?

Upgrading is simple! Just select the plan that best fits your needs, then follow the checkout process. Your premium features will be activated immediately after payment is confirmed.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new features will be available immediately. If you downgrade, your current plan will remain active until the end of your billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are securely processed through Stripe.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all premium plans. If you're not satisfied with your premium experience, you can request a full refund within the first 7 days of your subscription.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your conversation history and personal data will remain in your account even if you downgrade to the Basic plan. However, you will lose access to premium features and any content created exclusively with those features.
