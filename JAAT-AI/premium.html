Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and features

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We also support payment through PayPal and various regional payment methods.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time. Changes to your subscription will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with your premium plan, contact our support team within 7 days of purchase for a full refund.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No long-term contracts or commitments. All plans are subscription-based and can be canceled at any time. Annual plans are billed for the full year but still come with our 7-day refund policy.

Do you offer team or enterprise discounts?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for teams and enterprise customers. Contact our sales team at sales@jaat-ai.com for a custom quote based on your specific requirements.

What happens when I reach my usage limits?

When you reach your plan's limits, you can continue using the platform with basic functionality. Premium features will become available again when your limits reset or when you upgrade your plan.
